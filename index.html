Meet Archana – The Heart Behind Pav Paradise

Archana is a 29‑year‑old Malayali born and brought up in Pune, Maharashtra. After spending 9 years at Wipro as a Software Engineer, she kept nurturing a long‑held passion for cooking—often praised for her delicious, almost magical food.

When she moved to Finland, she noticed that local street food lacked the authentic Indian Pav. Determined to fix that, she started a mission to bake Pav at home just like in India. She experimented with different flours, yeast types, and baking times—testing countless combinations. After six months of persistence, she perfected her Pav in Helsinki.

Since then, Archana has baked approximately 5,000 Pav and now runs a successful cloud kitchen specializing in Pav‑based street food and Pav loaves. She also takes bulk orders and aims to expand Pav Paradise across Europe in the coming years.
